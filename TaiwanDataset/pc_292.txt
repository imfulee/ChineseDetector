The “Gen 4” basically means its PCIe 4.0 compatible. In other words the motherboard could theoretically support PCIe 4.0. The Z490 GODLIKE also features an onboard OLED display (Dynamic Dashboard 2) described as a “colorful panel indicating a status of personality”. For now the OLED screens are limited to very few flagship motherboards. The godlike is clearly one of such. According to KOMACHI the board also features dual Thunderbolt 3 slots and a combination of 10Gbe Aquantia and 2.5Gbe Ethernet ports.
