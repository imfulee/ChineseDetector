來源: https://t.ly/Rx3B 原文: AMD Radeon RX 6800XT 3DMark results show it beating the RTX 3080 João Silva 11 hours ago Featured Tech News Graphics Following the leak of the RTX 3070 3DMark results multiple sources got hold of the 3DMark scores of the Radeon RX 6800 XT. Ranging from Fire Strike benchmarks to Time Spy the Radeon RX 6800 XT beats the RTX 3080 in 3 out of 6 benchmarks offering fairly similar performance in two and losing in the raytracingcentric Port Royal. The 3DMark scores of the Radeon RX 6800 XT come from IgorsLab Wccftech and @KittyYYuko (aka Kitty Corgi). There is not much difference in the scores across all three sources leading us to believe that they shouldnt be far from the final results. Starting with Igors results he claimed that this data comes from two board partners that use both AMD and NVIDIA GPUs like MSI Gigabyte and Asus. Wccftechs leak had a similar structure to Igors including both percental and score differences. Yuko Yoshida claimed that the scores he shared were of an engineering sample of a Navi 21XTbased card which features 80 CUs and not 72 like previously leaks suggested. The following table puts all the scores from the different sources together for better visualisation and compares it with the RTX 3080 (values from the sources and @harukaze5719). Both 1440p benchmarks may not be a fair comparison because we dont know the CPU configuration of the system running the Radeon RX 6800 XT. We assume that the rest of the benchmarks were running on equivalent systems for comparison purposes. These results seem quite promising for the red team with Radeon RX 6800 XT only losing to the RTX 3080 in Port Royal which is a raytracing benchmark. On Time Spy scores are fairly similar but when moving on to Fire Strike benchmarks theres an improvement of over 20% compared to the RTX 3080. As per @harukaze5719s chart it even beats the RTX 3090 on Fire Strike Extreme at 1440p. AMD will officially announce its upcoming graphics cards on October 28th.
